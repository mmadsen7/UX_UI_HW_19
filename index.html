ALOHA. HOWDY. HELLO.

I'm Mikaela

I'm a UX/UI Designer and Graphic Designer. I've lived all over the U.S. and Canada but, I currently call beautiful Utah home. I love spending time outside in nature to balance out all my screen time. I love art, color, music, design, and typography. I enjoy reading, writing, copy editing, and researching. I love interviewing people and learning about their stories and experiences and designing solutions to solve their problems.





MY SKILLS

User Research

I was on the Journalism staff in middle school and high school and I later got a Minor in Journalism in University. Journalism taught me strong communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills. It also created a love for interviewing people and learning about their stories and experiences. I find it satisfing that through UX Design I can create solutions to solve user problems.

Design Thinking

I love working iteratively through all phases of the design process from ideation to solution. I enjoy taking the quantitative and qualitative data I find through research, analyzing the data and identifying user pain points to then create design goals to solve the user's needs.

User Interface Design

My background and degree is in Art and Graphic Design, so I enjoy making the user's visual experience a priority. Beautiful, crisp and concise design gets me excited. I believe great UI Design is functional, reliable, accessible, and delightful to use.
